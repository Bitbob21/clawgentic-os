Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need technical knowledge?
No. You only need a server (or we can provide one). Everything else is handled by Max.
What if I already have a server?
No problem. I install Founder OS on your existing server. Price stays the same — $1.500 one-time.
How does payment work?
Invoice via bank transfer or Lightning Address. 50% on kickoff, 50% after setup is complete.
Can I try first?
Yes. There's a 14-day trial period. After that, regular billing begins.
What exactly is Max?
Max is your AI Chief of Staff — an AI agent running on your server, accessible through your dashboard. No separate OpenAI subscription needed.
What happens after the workshop?
Every participant leaves with their own working system. After that, anyone can book add-ons individually or upgrade to a full Personal Setup.
