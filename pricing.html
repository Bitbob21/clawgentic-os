Frequently Asked Questions
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the end of your billing cycle.
What counts as a "teammate"?
Anyone with a login to your workspace. Viewers (read-only) are free and don't count toward your seat limit.
Is the free Starter plan really free forever?
Yes. Starter has hard limits (3 projects, 10 tasks/project) but there's no trial expiry. We won't ask for your credit card.
How does Lightning Address payments work?
Pro and Agency plans can be paid via Lightning Address (like max@clawgentic.os). We run our own LNbits node. Bank transfer and card also accepted.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is exportable as JSON at any time. After cancellation, data is retained for 30 days and then permanently deleted.
Do you offer refunds?
Monthly plans: no refunds after 7 days. Annual plans: prorated refund within 30 days. No questions asked.
